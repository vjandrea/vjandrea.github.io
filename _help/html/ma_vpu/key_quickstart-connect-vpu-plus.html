Quickstart Connect VPU plus

1 = 3 x DVI OUT

2 = Mains switch ON / OFF (In standby mode - switch on “I”, the AC power voltage is present within the instrument, even if the apparatus is switched off by the push button on the front)

3 = Mains Input (blue) 120 / 230 V, 50/60 Hz no switching of voltage necessary (Cable not included ex works)

4 = Mains Output (grey) 120 / 230 V, 50/60 Hz no switching of voltage necessary (Cable not included ex works)

5 = 2 x Ethernet (for connecting MA-consoles, grandMA onPC ....)

6 = SDI In 1 + 2 (option)

7= 2 x USB 3.0 + 2 more USB 2.0 at the front panel and 1 more USB 2.0 at the back panel

8 = Audio OUT left / right

9 = eSATA - port for connecting external drive

Use only cable with max length 0,5 m!

Do not disconnect drive when at work!
