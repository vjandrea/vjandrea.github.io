Intended use

The MA VPU (Video Processing Unit) is an element of the MA system family. Intended use is output of audio and video via DVI, Art-Net or sACN. Three different versions of the MA VPU are available to suit all needs: MA VPU plus, MA VPU basic and MA VPU light.

With a huge amount of functions, features and effects together with any console of the grandMA family these will be the most innovative tool available to control visual effects independent of the type of fixtures needing control.
