Assign Key

The key Assign is located in the command area on the right of the key Prvw.

 

  • Pressing Assign enters Assign keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> Assign
  • Pressing Assign Assign enters Label keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> Label
  • Pressing Assign Assign Assign enters Appearance keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> Appearance
