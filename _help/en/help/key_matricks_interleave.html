MAtricks Interleave

This topic illustrates how to use MAtricksInterleave based on an example. 

For information on the keyword see MAtricksInterleave. 

Requirement:

  • Fixtures are selected via the command line or keys.

For more information on how to select fixtures for the usage of MAtricks see MAtricks.

Example

Initial situation:

  1. To apply the MAtricksInterleave, tap the button on the left of the  sign displayed below Interleave in the MAtricks toolbar. 

For more information on the toolbar see MAtricks toolbar.

  1. The calculator opens.
    -Enter 4.
  2. Press Please. 

-or-

-Use the command line as follows:

[Channel]> MAtricksInterleave 4

-Press Please.  

The fixtures are selected in an interval of 4. 

  1. To jump to the adjacent fixture holding the interval of 4, press Next. 

  1. To jump to the next fixture, press Next. 

​​​​

  1. To reselect the fixtures, simultaneously press Prev and Next. 

  1. To move through the selection in the interval of 4, press and hold  and press Next. 

  1. To move further through the selection, press and hold  and press Next.

  1. To reselect all rows, press and hold and simultaneously press Prev and Next. 

Hint:
To jump to the third fixture in the second row:
1. Press Next Next Next.
2. Press and hold and press Next.
3. Press and hold and press Next.


  1. To disable the MAtricksInterleave, press and hold Set and simultaneously press Prev and Next. 

The MAtricksInterleave is now disabled.
