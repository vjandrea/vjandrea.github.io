<<< [GoFastBack] Key

The key <<<  [GoFastBack] is located in the command area on the left of the key Learn.

 

  • Pressing <<< enters <<< [GoFastBack] keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> <<<
  • Pressing <<< <<< enters Black keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> Black
  • Pressing <<< <<< <<< enters ToZero keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> ToZero
