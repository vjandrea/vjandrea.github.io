Store Key

The key Store is located in the command area on the left of the key

.

 

  • Pressing Store enters the Store keyword into the command line and changes the encoder bar to the store encoder bar.

 

[Channel]> Store

 

 

  • Pressing and holding Store opens the pop-up Temporary Store Options.

 


 

  • Pressing and holding  and Store enters the Record keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> Record
