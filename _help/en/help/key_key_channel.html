Channel Key

The key Channel is located on the left of the key Fixture.


 

  • Pressing Channel enters the Channel keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> Channel

 

  • Pressing Channel Channel enters the Dmx keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> Dmx

 

  • Pressing Channel Channel Channel enters the DmxUniverse keyword into the command line.
[Channel]> DmxUniverse
